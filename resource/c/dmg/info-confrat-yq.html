泰国，一个神奇的国度，拥有佛国风情的寺庙、浪漫迷人的岛屿、独特的历史文化、特色美食和按摩、现代化的繁华都市，以及成就了“微笑之国”盛誉的热情友善的人民。泰国一直以来被称为暹罗（xiānluó），1939年才正式更名为泰王国。地处中南半岛中部,首都曼谷。“泰”，主要是取其“自由”之意。泰国是佛教之国，佛教徒占全国人口九成五以上。作为东南亚第二大经济体，泰国GDP中的60%依赖于出口，其主要出口产品包括农产品、纺织品、橡胶、汽车、电子电器，珠宝等。同时，泰国也是世界上首屈一指的旅游胜地之一。

Thailand is a wondrous kingdom, featuring Buddhist temples, exotic wildlife, and spectacular islands. Along with its fascinating history, unique culture, delectable Thai food and massage, Thailand is also well-known for its modern cities and friendly people that epitomize Thailand’s “Land of Smiles” reputation. Thailand is located in central Indochina. It was recognized as Siam until 1939 when it officially became the Kingdom of Thailand, which stands for the "Land of Freedom". Capital city is Bangkok and nearly 95% of its population believes in Buddhism. As the 2nd largest economy system in Southeast Asia, approximately 60% of Thailand’s GDP is dependent on export, including agricultural products, textiles, rubber, automobiles, electronic and electrical products and jewelries. In addition, Thailand is one of the premier tourist destinations in the world.
